Privacy Policy for dinogame.onl

Data Collection

At DinoGame.onl, we only collect minimal gameplay data to enhance your experience:

  • We record game scores for global ranking purposes
  • No personal information (names, emails, IP addresses, etc.) is collected or stored
  • All data is anonymous and cannot be linked to individual users

About the Game

The dinosaur game (also known as Chrome Dino or T-Rex Runner) was originally developed by Google Chrome as part of their browser. We've created dinogame.onl to:

  • Improve gameplay mechanics
  • Add global score rankings
  • Enhance visual elements
All intellectual property rights for the original game belong to Google LLC.

Third-Party Services

We use simple cookies to:

  • Remember your high score locally (stored only on your device)
  • Maintain game preferences

No tracking cookies or analytics services are implemented.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Significant changes will be announced on our website.
